# Seed data

`seed/` is the founding dataset: **2,095 adaptive sports organizations** compiled from **22
authoritative sources** (Move United, NWBA, CAF, U.S. Paralympics, and 18 others), with full
per-organization source provenance in `organization_data_sources.csv`. Compiled January 2026;
the pipeline's job is to keep it alive from here.
